How to access Information and Log Grievance

The Ministry has put in place a citizen-friendly, strengthened Grievance Redressal System to ensure quick redressal of Public Grievance. The following mechanism, which has been set up for dissemination of information about various services, handling of grievance and citizen feedback, may be accessed by citizens to register their Grievance:-

Passport Seva Portal

The Passport Seva Portal [(www.passportindia.gov.in)](/psp) provides information related to documents/ formalities to be completed for online submission of passport application. In case of any other query, Click here to log a request for information

![](/psp/static/media/Vector.8c395c215b91f0bb7f648eda0aeb9227.svg)

National Call Centre

There is a National Call Centre with a toll free number 1800-258-1800, which operates in 17 languages on 24x7 basis to provide comprehensive information related to application submission

![](/psp/static/media/1.2.ae07e99de0973563c8c5.png)

E-mail / Call to respective Passport Office (PO)

The Passport Seva Portal [(www.passportindia.gov.in)](/psp) can also be accessed for getting details of each PO whereby applicant can directly be in touch with them through e-mail/telephone/fax. For contact details of the Passport offices Click here .

![](/psp/static/media/Vector.8c395c215b91f0bb7f648eda0aeb9227.svg)

HelpDesk

There is also an e-mail (helpdesk@passportindia.gov.in) based helpdesk which can be accessed by citizens through the Passport Seva Portal [(www.passportindia.gov.in)](/psp)

Passport Seva Mobile Application

Smartphone users can access information using mobile app ‘Passport Seva’. It is available on Android and iOS

![](/psp/static/media/Vector.8c395c215b91f0bb7f648eda0aeb9227.svg)

Short Message Service (SMS)

Applicant can send an SMS query at any time to know the current status of the application. The applicant can track the status of his/her application by sending an SMS to 9704100100 in the format for example, STATUS BNG071268435013.
