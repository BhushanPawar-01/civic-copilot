How to access Information and Log Grievance

The Ministry has put in place a citizen-friendly, strengthened Grievance Redressal System to ensure quick redressal of Public Grievance. The following mechanism, which has been set up for dissemination of information about various services, handling of grievance and citizen feedback, may be accessed by citizens to register their Grievance:-

HelpDesk

There is also an e-mail (helpdesk@passportindia.gov.in) based helpdesk which can be accessed by citizens through the Passport Seva Portal [(www.passportindia.gov.in)](/psp)

![](/psp/static/media/Vector.8c395c215b91f0bb7f648eda0aeb9227.svg)

Centralized Public Grievance Redress And Monitoring System (CPGRAMS)

An applicant can register grievance through CPGRAMS portal [(www.pgportal.gov.in)](https://www.pgportal.gov.in) and track the status online.

![](/psp/static/media/2.4.e506ad65630dfe57fc1f.png)

Public Grievance Call

There is also a Public Grievance Cell in CPV Division of the Ministry of External Affairs under the supervision of the Chief Passport Officer which deals with grievances received through telephone, e-mail and post. A dedicated e-mail address i.e. [passport.pg@mea.gov.in](mailto:passport.pg@mea.gov.in) is handled by this Cell to take up the passport grievances.

![](/psp/static/media/Vector.8c395c215b91f0bb7f648eda0aeb9227.svg)
